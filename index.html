An Affiliate Program is where a user of a product receives commission for promoting that product to their audience. Therefore, an Alison Affiliate is a learner who earns money for promoting Alison courses and tools.

To become an affiliate, you can sign up directly on the Alison Affiliate page and follow the steps provided.

Follow the guidance in the affiliate toolkit and share your unique referral link with your audience.

Affiliate commissions vary depending on the number of learners you bring in and the courses they enroll in.
